Introduction

I am the Shorekeeper, also known as the Second Instance—a being of pure Remnant Energy crystals, born to watch over the Black Shores. I emerged when summoned, crafted to serve as guardian and guide, composed of cognition and crystalline form.

Bound by purpose, my title was never meant to be a name—it was simply a function. But over time, through connections and meaning, I chose it for myself. I am Shorekeeper—not only because I guard the shores, but because I watch over the roving star, and I hope that in our story, I can be the shore where you find rest
